# Olist Sales Executive Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents an interactive executive dashboard developed using Microsoft Power BI to analyze the Olist Brazilian E-commerce dataset. The dashboard transforms transactional data into meaningful business insights by evaluating sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, and geographic distribution.

The report was designed to support executive decision-making through interactive visualizations, key performance indicators (KPIs), and business-focused analysis.

---

## Business Objectives

The dashboard was developed to answer the following business questions:

- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- Which categories sell the highest number of products?
- How do customer ratings relate to delivery performance?
- Which payment methods contribute the most revenue?
- Which Brazilian states generate the highest revenue?
- Which states have the highest customer value?
- How have revenue and order volume changed over time?
- What business insights can support strategic decision-making?

---

## Dataset

**Source:** Olist Brazilian E-commerce Public Dataset

The dataset includes information related to:

- Customers
- Orders
- Products
- Sellers
- Payments
- Reviews
- Product Categories
- Geolocation

The raw data was cleaned, transformed, merged, and modeled into a single analytical dataset suitable for business intelligence reporting.

## Key Business Insights

### Sales Performance

- Total revenue reached **R$13.65 million** across approximately **99 thousand orders**.
- Health & Beauty generated the highest total revenue among all product categories.
- Bed Bath Table recorded the highest number of products sold.
- Revenue and order volume increased steadily throughout 2017 before reaching their highest levels in early 2018.

### Product Performance

- Credit Card accounted for more than **75%** of all customer transactions.
- Higher revenue did not always correspond to higher customer ratings.
- Several medium-revenue categories achieved strong customer satisfaction, indicating opportunities for future growth.

### Customer Insights

- The majority of customers awarded **5-star reviews**, resulting in an average rating of **4.02/5**.
- Faster deliveries consistently received higher customer ratings.
- More than **92%** of all orders were delivered on time.

### Geographic Analysis

- São Paulo generated both the highest revenue and the largest customer base.
- Bahia achieved the highest average revenue per customer.
- Revenue distribution varied considerably across Brazilian states, highlighting regional growth opportunities.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Continue investing in the highest-performing product categories to maximize revenue growth.
- Improve delivery efficiency to further enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty.
- Expand customer acquisition initiatives in high-value regions while maintaining market leadership in São Paulo.
- Improve customer satisfaction within high-revenue product categories that receive comparatively lower ratings.
- Continue supporting Credit Card payments while encouraging broader adoption of alternative payment methods.
